BDEP: Scatter lower bits into positions selected by bitmask

BDEP Zd.H, Zn.H, Zm.H (SVE2+BitPerm+NS
svuint16_t svbdep[_u16](svuint16_t op1, svuint16_t op2)

128-bit SVE

Within each 16-bit lane, deposit the least-significant bits from (1) at the positions in (3) indicated by the non-zero bits from (2), zeroing all other bits in (3). The order of the bits is preserved.
