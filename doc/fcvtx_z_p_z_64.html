FCVTX: Floating-point down convert, rounding to odd (predicated)

FCVTX Zd.S, Pg/M, Zn.D (SVE2 (SME
svfloat32_t svcvtx_f32[_f64]_m(svfloat32_t inactive, svbool_t pg, svfloat64_t op)

128-bit SVE
