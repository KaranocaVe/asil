SCVTF: Signed integer convert to floating-point (predicated)

SCVTF Zd.S, Pg/M, Zn.D (SVE (SME
svfloat32_t svcvt_f32[_s64]_m(svfloat32_t inactive, svbool_t pg, svint64_t op)

128-bit SVE

Convert each signed 64-bit integer from (1) to a 32-bit float, writing the results to the even lanes of (2), zeroing odd lanes. The current rounding mode selected by FPCR is used.
