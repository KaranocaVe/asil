FCVTNT: Floating-point down convert and narrow (top, predicated)

128-bit SVE

Convert each 64-bit float from (1) to a 32-bit float, writing the results to the odd lanes of (3), preserving the even lanes from (2). The current rounding mode selected by FPCR is used.
