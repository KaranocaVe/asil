FRINTA: Floating-point round to integral value (predicated)

FRINTA Zd.H, Pg/M, Zn.H (SVE (SME
svfloat16_t svrinta[_f16]_m(svfloat16_t inactive, svbool_t pg, svfloat16_t op)

128-bit SVE
