UCVTF: Unsigned integer convert to floating-point (predicated)

UCVTF Zd.H, Pg/M, Zn.D (SVE (SME
svfloat16_t svcvt_f16[_u64]_m(svfloat16_t inactive, svbool_t pg, svuint64_t op)

128-bit SVE

Convert each unsigned 64-bit integer from (1) to a 16-bit float, writing the results to the lowest overlapping 16-bit lanes of (2), and zeroing other lanes. The current rounding mode selected by FPCR is used.
